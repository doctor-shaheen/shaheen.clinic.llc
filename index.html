Ahmed Shaheen, MD

Founder & CEO | Clinical & Computational Neuroscience

Dr. Ahmed Shaheen is a medical doctor and researcher specializing in computational neuroscience. His work focuses on leveraging machine learning, deep learning, and advanced statistical techniques to understand brain function and develop innovative solutions for neurological disorders.

With an h-index of 15 and over 60 peer-reviewed publications, Dr. Shaheen bridges the gap between computational methods and clinical practice. He serves as Academic Editor for PLOS ONE and Editorial Board Member for Digital Health, while leading multicentric collaborative research studies globally.

Dr. Shaheen is passionate about education, teaching statistical analysis, machine learning, and R/Python programming to medical researchers worldwide through the Shaheen Clinic educational platform.
